Cake Cookies

Description

A sandwich is a food generally consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between sliced bread.

Ingredient

  • Bread
  • Spread
  • Filling
  • Garnish

Steps

  1. Slather your condiment on the bottom slice of bread
  2. Add the densest vegetables, followed by lettuce and then tomatoes
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the meat
  5. Spread another layer of condiment on the top slice of bread
